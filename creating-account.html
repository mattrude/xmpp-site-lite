New User Guide

XMPP (formerly known as Jabber) is an instant messaging system that is, free, standard, and open; and allows you to chat live with your family, friends and professional contacts, and be warned of the presence of your contacts, while keeping your independence vis-à vis-à-vis your software and service providers.

This page is here to help a new user, register an account, download the needed software, and log into the server.
